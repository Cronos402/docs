**Endpoint:** `https://facilitator.cronoslabs.org/v2/x402`

The Cronos facilitator enables **gasless USDC.e payments** on the Cronos blockchain using EIP-3009 `transferWithAuthorization`. Users sign a permit off-chain, and the facilitator executes the transfer on-chain, paying the gas fees.

- **Instant Settlement**: Payments are verified and settled on-chain in real-time
- **Production Ready**: Official infrastructure from Cronos Labs

1. **Client Signs Permit**: User signs an EIP-3009 `transferWithAuthorization` message
2. **Verify Endpoint**: Client calls `/verify` to validate the permit
3. **Settle Endpoint**: Client calls `/settle` to execute the on-chain transfer
4. **Payment Confirmed**: Facilitator returns proof of payment for retry
